Marbling is the hallmark of a great ribeye steak. It ensures that the meat is not only tender but also succulent and richly flavored. As the fat melts during cooking, it bastes the meat from within, creating a luscious, mouthwatering texture and a depth of flavor that leaves an indelible mark on the palate.
