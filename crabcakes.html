Cost: R150

Golden-brown patties

The origins of crab cakes can be traced to the shores of the Atlantic and Gulf coasts of the United States, where the abundance of crabs made them a natural choice for local cuisine. Early versions of crab cakes often featured simple ingredients like crabmeat, breadcrumbs, and seasonings. Over time, these humble seafood patties evolved, adapting to regional variations, and became a symbol of coastal living and indulgence.

Ingredients:
