Deviled eggs, often referred to as "stuffed eggs," have a long and storied history. The concept of hard-boiling and filling eggs dates back to ancient Rome, but the term "deviled" began to emerge in the 18th century. It refers to the use of spicy or zesty ingredients to flavor the egg yolks. Over time, the dish evolved to include a variety of ingredients, making it a beloved staple on appetizer menus.
